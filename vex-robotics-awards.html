Vex Robotics Excellence Awards

Flying Penguins wins the Vex Robotics Excellence Award at the Vex Robotics Dublin Dec 2025.

This prestigious award recognizes teams that demonstrate overall excellence in building a well-rounded VEX Robotics program. The Excellence Award is the highest honor presented in the VEX Robotics Competition.

This award qualifies us to compete in US Open Vex Robotics Championship 2026 and California States Vex Robotics Championship 2026.
