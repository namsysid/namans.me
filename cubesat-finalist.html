MIT Build a CubeSat Finalist, Best Demo Award

I am proud to announce that our team was selected as a finalist for the MIT Build a CubeSat competition and has won the Best Demo Award! My team (Soundwave) qualified for the finals and won the best demo award for the MIT Build a CubeSat Challenge, where we had to design a CubeSat to assist the astronauts on the Artemis mission. We developed, prototyped, and tested a satellite to detect mid-sized meteor impacts on the moon by observing changes in dust packing.
