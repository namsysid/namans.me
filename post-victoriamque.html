SJ Youth Orchestra performs Post Victoriamque!

My composition "Post Victoriamque" was performed by the SJ Youth Orchestra at the SJ Youth Orchestra Concert on Nov 2025 at Oclef Center.

Post Victoriamque is a musical composition that celebrates achievement and triumph. The piece was written to commemorate significant accomplishments and the journey that leads to victory.
