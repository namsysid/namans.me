SBReader

SBReader is an app that mimics live science bowl competitions, with scoring, multiple choice support, timers, and an AI question judge. It also contains AI study assists.

SBReader provides a comprehensive platform for science bowl practice and competition simulation. The application features real-time scoring, multiple choice question support, built-in timers, and an AI-powered question judge that can evaluate answers and provide feedback.

In addition to competition simulation, SBReader includes AI study assists to help users prepare for science bowl competitions more effectively.
